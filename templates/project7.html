Anti Bullying and Mental Health Awareness Program 1 – LACAS School , Johar Town

On 26th and 27th February(2026), Minimo Cares conducted an Anti-Bullying and Mental Health Awareness Project at LACAS School, Johar Town with Grade 4 and 5 students. The session focused on helping young students understand the serious effects of bullying, how to identify bullying behavior, and the importance of seeking help when faced with such situations. The students were highly responsive, curious, and actively engaged throughout the session. Our psychologist guided the children on how to speak up, seek support, and report bullying to their safety officers or trusted adults. Through discussions and interactive activities, the students gained valuable awareness about kindness, empathy, and mental well-being. The school appreciated the session and its impact, and we were truly glad to have the opportunity to conduct this meaningful awareness program.
